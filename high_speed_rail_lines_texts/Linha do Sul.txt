Linha do Sul is a Portuguese railway line which connects Campolide A, in Lisbon, and Tunes, in the Algarve. The first section, from Pinhal Novo to SetÃºbal, was opened in 1861.[1] The route to Funcheira opened on 25 May 1920.[2] In 2003 it was linked to Lisbon, crossing the Tagus River on the 25 de Abril Bridge.[3]
